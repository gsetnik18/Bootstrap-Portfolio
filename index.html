About Me



My name is Gregory; pleased to meet you! I am a fledgling web developer, historical fencer, tabletop and video gamer, cooking enthusiast and all- around nerd. I am 26 years old, have three siblings, and prefer dogs over cats. Since this is not a dating profile, I won't bore you with any further personal details; allow me to show you my professional qualifications!

It wasn't until late 2018 that I decided to stop wasting my time doing unfulfilling work, so I turned to code. I am very excited to see what the world of programming has to offer!
